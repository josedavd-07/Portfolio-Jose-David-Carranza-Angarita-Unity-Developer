Projects

Tiempos Pasados

"One minute to leave behind what haunts you. Can you make it in time?" This game is a short but intense experience about the weight of the past and the inner struggle to let go. In a world covered in mist, each step is uncertain until you decide to face what holds you back. Activating the monuments, you dissipate the fog of guilt and reproach, clearing the path to clarity.

Parasite Invasion

You find yourself inside the human body piloting an elite ship designed to eradicate viruses and parasites. However, a threat looms: waves of hostile microorganisms have invaded the system, firing infectious projectiles that drain the body's life. You, as the pilot, are the last line of defense. Your mission is clear: dodge, attack, and survive.

DayDream

Chaotic 3D shooter with exaggerated physics and intentional glitches. Dive into a surreal world full of action and chaos. In Daydream, nothing is stable: weapons behave unpredictably, shots defy physics, and levels are designed to challenge and disorient you at every turn. Inspired by classic shooters, but with a modern, glitchy twist.
